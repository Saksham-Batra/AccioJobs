The Beagle Blog


The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much
larger foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as
Beagling. Posessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the
beagle is a primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural
imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is inteliigent
and is a popular pet due to its size , good temper , and a lack of inherited heath
problems.

Trivia

  1. Height - 13–16 in (33-41 cm)
  2. Weight - 22–25 lb (10.0-11.3 kg)
  3. Coat - Short haired, hard coat of medium length
  4. Color - Tricolor or white in combination with black & tan/brown or brown/tan
  5. Lifespan - 12–15 years
